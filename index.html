An Overview of Australian Wheat Production

Australia has been producing wheat for many years.

Australian Statistics

Australia's land has become more productive over time due to increased fertiliser use and advances in equipment.

Wheat is Australia's highest value crop, worth over 12.3 Billion AUD in 2020.

Australia's Wheat Compared Globally

Australian wheat production is predicted to decrease more than any other global region in 2021 and 2023, while reaching similar levels to 2020 in 2020. An explanation for this may be the recent increase in production of other agricultural products such as canola and barley.

In 2020, Australia was the 9th largest producer of wheat, growing more than 2% of wheat globally, equivalent to 33kt.
